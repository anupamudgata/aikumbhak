Breathe safely 🌬️

Please read before your first session.

Kumbhak is a wellness tool, not medical advice. Breath retention and paced breathing can cause lightheadedness.
  • Practice sitting or lying down — never while driving, in water, or swimming.
  • Stop immediately if you feel dizzy, faint, or short of breath.
  • Consult a doctor first if you are pregnant or have heart conditions, epilepsy, or high blood pressure.
  • Never force a breath-hold. Comfort over duration, always.
